Fake Love

RM opened up about the true meaning of "Fake Love" and how
it actually fits into a bigger story that the group is trying
to tell through their music. "Basically, our albums are like a
four-part series; [the series] is about love. So [Love Yourself: Tear]
is part three and it's like a turning point. We're saying that if you're
not true to yourself, your love won't last forever. The love could be
person to person, it could be between me and myself," he explained. He
then joked, "It's kinda deep." And like, it kind of is though.
