Blood Sweat & Tears

On the single “Blood Sweat & Tears” from the group’s 2016
album, Wings, tropical house blends with moombahton production
as members Jimin, Jungkook, Suga, RM, J-Hope, V, and Jin declare
their willingness to throw everything on the line for love, success
or whatever their aim might be. Thematically, the guys blend sentiments
of temptation with an often carnal willingness to sacrifice everything.
