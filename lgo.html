Life Goes On

This nostalgic song blurs alternative hip-hop sound with
acoustic guitar and synth-pop. The lyrics offer comfort in a
time when the world faces the pandemic.
The story behind the song begins from BTS declaring that “even
in the face of this new normality, our life goes on and imparts
a message of healing to fans and to the world.”
The theme “life goes on” was mentioned several times while BTS
shared the process behind the album through vlogs over the months.
