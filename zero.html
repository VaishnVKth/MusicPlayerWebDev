00:00 (Zero O'Clock)

00:00 (Zero O'Clock) is a song by Jungkook, Jimin, Jin and V
(also known as the vocal-line) of BTS. It was released on
February 21, 2020, and appears as the thirteenth track in their
fourth studio album Map of the Soul: 7. Lyrically, it is about
reminding everyone to stay happy no matter what they are going
through and that everyday is a fresh start.
